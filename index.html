Grid Scout ASU Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Grid Scout ASU collects the following information to provide our service:

  • Authentication information (Google Sign-In)
  • Location data (when using the app's geolocation features)
  • Work order and component information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and manage access
  • Track component locations and maintenance
  • Process work orders and maintain records

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.
